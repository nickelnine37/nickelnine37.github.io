but is it data science?

When the flush of a new-born sun fell first on Eden's green and blue,
Our father Adam sat under the Tree and downloaded Python 3.7.2,
And made a colorful diagram about their fresh fruit overeliance,
Till the Devil whispered behind the leaves, "It's pretty, but is it Data Science?"

Wherefore he called to his wife, and fled to fashion his work anew --
He installed Pandas, Numpy, even Scipy as a result of this dread review;
He wrapped it in a conda environment -- and that was a glorious gain
When the Devil chuckled "Is it Data Science?" in the ear of his son Cain.

They fought and they talked in the North and the South, they talked and they fought in the West,
Adam learned the central limit theorem and how to hypothesis test --
"Look, I know how to derive the eviedence lower bound", said Adam in defiance,
And the Devil bubbled below the keel: "It's statistics, but is it Data Science?"

We have learned about neural networks and dimensionality reduction,
We have learned the importance of using comments in code used in production,
We know about GDPR and regulatory data compliance;
But the Devil whoops, as he whooped of old: "It's clever, but is it Data Science?"

And when the flicker of London sun falls on the open-plan startup hub,
The sons of Adam sit them down and open their Macbook pros up --
They install this and download that on their shiny digital appliance,
But the Devil mutters behind the bean bags: "It's pretty, but is it Data Science?"
